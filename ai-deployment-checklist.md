# AI Deployment Checklist for HR Operations

*Before you go live — what must be in place, by quadrant.*

**Audience:** HR practitioners, HRBPs, HR operations leads

-----

> **How to Use This:** This checklist is for after you have selected a tool and before you go live. The [Vendor Scorecard](ai-vendor-scorecard.md) covers procurement. This covers deployment — the governance, communication, and oversight structure that must exist before your first user session.
